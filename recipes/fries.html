How to make French Fries

Here is a simple recipe-

  1. Peel and cut potatoes into thin strips.
  2. Soak in water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  3. Heat oil (375°F/190°C) in a deep fryer or pan.
  4. Dry potatoes thoroughly and fry in batches for 3-4 minutes, until golden.
  5. Remove fries, drain on paper towels, and season with salt.
  6. Optional: For extra crispiness, fry a second time for 2-3 minutes.

Serve hot with ketchup or your favorite dipping sauce!
