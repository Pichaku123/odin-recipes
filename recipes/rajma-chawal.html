How to make Rajma Chawal

For a quick Rajma Chawal recipe-

  1. Soak 1 cup of kidney beans overnight. Pressure cook with water and a pinch of salt for 3-4 whistles until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat oil, saut√© 1 chopped onion, 2 chopped tomatoes, and 1 teaspoon each of ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, and chili powder.
  3. Add cooked beans and some water. Simmer for 15 minutes. Adjust seasoning.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice (chawal). Enjoy!
